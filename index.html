News2PubMed: Linking Health News to Medical Literature

Note: our search engine is updated with new literature every night, so for journal papers published exactly today, our demo system may not be able to find it.
STEP 1. Install our browser extension News2PubMed from Chrome web store.
During installing the extension, if you see ``this extension can read and change your data on sites'', that means the extension will insert a button of Search Health Literature into the news page your are reading on news-medical, eurekalert, or reuters.
STEP 2. For a quick start, click a news article below, then click Search Health Literature under the headline (see the picture below).
References
(1) Jun Wang and Bei Yu (2021) News2PubMed: A Browser Extension for Linking Health News to Medical Literature. In ACM SIGIR'21, July 11–15, 2021, Virtual Event, Canada. pp 2605--2609. https://doi.org/10.1145/3404835.3462788
(2) Jun Wang and Bei Yu (2021) Linking Health News to Research Literature. https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.06472 (Technical details.)
